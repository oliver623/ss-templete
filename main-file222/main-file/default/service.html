Fulfillment Service

We are a leading provider of third party logistics focused on e-commerce industry. We offer a complete suite of fulfillment services including picking, labeling, packing, shipping and more, to move your goods and merchandise from our warehouses into your clients’ hands quickly, accurately, and efficiently.

Order Management

Our simplified e-commerce platform integration, developed and maintained by our own R&D team, allows you to manage and download latest orders near real time from websites like Amazon, Walmart, Wayfair and more, making us the one-stop solution for both order management and fulfillment.

Inventory Management

As your logistics partner, we have valuable experience learned from working with many e-commerce companies for years, with which we can help you manage your stock with best practices of the industry. Our digitalized workflow ensures that every single record of inventory update is accurate and trackable.

Return &Refurbish Management

Return is one of the stages of e-commerce life cycles and is also called reverse logistics. The management of returns can often affect your margins. We accept and process returned items for you, from small items like clothing and shoes to high-value electronic devices such as computers and printers. With our refurbish service, undamaged and lightly damaged products will soon get available for purchase again.

Shipping Service

We make sure you receive the most affordable pricing possible for the delivery speed your customers choose by comparing costs from a variety of shipping service providers and selecting the nearest warehouse to fulfill. Carriers such as UPS, Fedex, USPS pick up from our fulfillment centers and you can choose whichever you prefer.

Customization Service

Every business has its core values and unique requirements. With a strong and professional IT team, we provide electronic product assembly and customization service. Besides, we’re always ready if you need other kinds of customization services for your products.
