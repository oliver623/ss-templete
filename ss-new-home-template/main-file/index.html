About Us

Established in 2015, Speeder Solutions has built its reputation on excellence in shipping and logistics, and expertise in return and repair, with a constant focus on serving our customers. Our fulfillment service was created to help business like yours get dependable, excellent shipping from loading dock to customers.

Why choose us?

Distributed Inventory

Empowered by our warehouses strategically located across the country, you can distribute your inventory and offer competitive two-day shipping to your customers and tremendously reduce shipping cost.

Competitive shipping price

We offer shipping labels on our platform at a competitive price through our partnership with carriers like UPS, Fedex, USPS and so on. Save shipping cost to make your products more affordable and attractive to your customers.

Professional Warehouse

We take it very seriously to ensure your products are secure and in good condition. With safety facilities installed we monitor every corner of our warehouses to protect your products.

Cloud-based System

We’re 100% on cloud and aimed to provide a service always online. Stop worrying about system failures in peak seasons and we can handle it perfectly. Enabled by cloud facilities, we’re guaranteed to provide you with accurate and up to date inventory data.

Simple Platform Integration

Connect your stores on Amazon, Walmart, Shopify and other platforms with very simple steps to get integrated. New orders to fulfill get synchronized to us nearly real-time and start enjoying our one-stop fulfillment service.

Passionate customer service

Our fulfillment and logistics experts are always passionate and committed to provide professional customer services. We care about the customer experience as much as you and strive to be an extension of your team.
